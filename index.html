01 — ABOUT

A perspective forged in the real world.

I grew up in Mariupol, Ukraine — a city whose name now evokes both devastation and defiance. In 2022, I experienced the war firsthand. That experience became the intellectual and moral foundation of everything I pursue.

Now studying Political Science and Economics at the University of Pittsburgh, I work at the intersection of international law, diplomacy, and conflict analysis — combining academic inquiry with a voice that carries the weight of lived experience.
